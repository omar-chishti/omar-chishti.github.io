White Matter Tract Explorer

Overview

Interactive 3D visualization of white matter pathways in the human brain. This project presents 24 major fiber tracts derived from high-resolution diffusion MRI tractography data, courtesy of the Human Connectome Project.

Controls

  • Rotate: Click and drag to orbit
  • Zoom: Scroll wheel or pinch gesture
  • Pan: Right-click and drag (two-finger drag on mobile)
