White Matter Tract Explorer

Overview

Interactive 3D visualization of white matter pathways in the human brain. This project visualizes 25+ major fiber tracts derived from diffusion MRI tractography data from the Human Connectome Project.

Controls

  • Rotate: Click and drag
  • Zoom: Scroll or pinch
  • Pan: Right-click and drag

Keyboard Shortcuts

  • i or ? - Open/close this info
  • Esc - Close modal
  • d - Toggle dark mode
  • b - Toggle brain overlay
